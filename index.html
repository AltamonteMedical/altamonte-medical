Executive Physical Pricing

Sample premium preventive care structure.

This is a website-ready sample pricing layout. Final pricing should be reviewed based on your market, contracted vendors, and whether coronary CT scanning is billed through an outside imaging center.

Tier 1
Starting at $795

Executive Physical

Extended physician visit, comprehensive history review, preventive planning, medication review, and personalized follow-up recommendations.
Tier 2
Starting at $1,295

Executive Physical Plus

Includes the executive physical, expanded preventive counseling, seca body composition scanning, and a more detailed written wellness plan.
Tier 3
Starting at $1,795+

Executive Cardiometabolic Package

Includes the executive physical plus coronary CT scan coordination (included in this tier), with optional add-on imaging services available. If the coronary CT is performed by an outside imaging center, the imaging fee can be billed separately or included as a bundled premium package.
Pricing note: coronary CT scanning is often performed through a third-party imaging facility, so many practices either list it as an add-on or bundle it into a higher-tier executive package depending on local pricing and vendor relationships.
